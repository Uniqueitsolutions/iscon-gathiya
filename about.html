about

Iscon Gathiya

We serve the real taste of Kathiawadi snacks, and you will only find them at our branches. Our roots date back to 2008. We have multiple outlets across different districts. Our outlets are in Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, Vadodara, and Surendranagar. We started as a modest Gathiya place, and today it stands as one of the most powerful snacking establishments in Gujarat. We prioritize your health and well-being. We do not use any artificial colors or preservatives in our dishes, and we follow all government rules and regulations for food safety and hygiene.

our

journey

Our journey began in 2008 with a small food cart and a big dream in Bapungar. Our first goal was to create Farsan, which captures the true essence of its origin. Over the years, we have continued to strive for excellence in everything, including a wide variety of dishes. We are thrilled to have over 10+ outlets, and we are still expanding them. We remain dedicated to the values of authenticity, quality, and customer service.
