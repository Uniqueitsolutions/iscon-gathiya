about us

In the tapestry of time, Iscon Gathiya has woven a remarkable journey spanning 15 splendid years, led by the visionary Mandeep Patel and embraced by our dedicated team. Our mission to deliver the authentic essence of Gujarati farsan, a culinary artistry that dances on the palate. With unwavering devotion to quality, we've elevated the experience of Kathiawadi snacks to an art form. From humble beginnings as a Gathiya place, we've blossomed into Gujarat's most influential snacking destination, gracing Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, Vadodara, and Surendranagar with our presence. Menu, a canvas of flavors, invites you to embark on a gastronomic odyssey where tradition and innovation harmonize. Your satisfaction, our masterpiece. For 15 years, we've stood as torchbearers of authentic Kathiawadi snacks, found exclusively in our Ahmedabad branches. Our roots, deeply planted in Iscon Circle, S.G Highway, have grown into a testament of enduring excellence.
